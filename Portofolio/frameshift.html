FRAME//SHIFT Festival

Brand Identity · Event Design · Graphic Design

Concept Project

2026

FRAME//SHIFT is a fictional animation and motion design festival focused on experimental visual storytelling and emerging digital artists.

The project explores how a bold visual identity can translate across multiple event applications — from posters and tickets to badges and social media graphics.

The goal was to design a cohesive festival system that feels dynamic, contemporary and visually engaging for a young creative audience.
