Character Animation · Lip Sync · Motion Graphics · Layout · Compositing · Final Edit

Internship

2025

During this internship, I produced a series of 7 educational animated episodes for children, each focused on introducing a letter of the alphabet through character-driven storytelling.

Each episode (2–4 minutes long) required building clear visual narratives tailored for young audiences — balancing simplicity, rhythm and expressive performance.

I was responsible for the full production pipeline, from asset preparation to character animation, lip sync, motion design, layout construction and final editing in After Effects.

PROCESS

Character Animation & Lip Sync

I animated expressive 2D characters with a focus on clarity and emotional readability for children. Lip sync and timing were carefully crafted to maintain rhythm and engagement across short episodic formats.

Motion Design & Visual Effects

I developed animated transitions, letter reveals and supporting motion systems that enhanced learning while maintaining playful energy. Motion was used as a storytelling tool, not just decoration.

Layout & Full Editing

Each episode was structured for narrative clarity, balancing pacing, repetition and visual hierarchy. Final edits were optimized for smooth delivery and platform-ready export.

Asset Preparation & Workflow

I prepared and optimized illustration assets for animation, structured compositions efficiently and maintained a clean, scalable After Effects workflow across all 7 episodes.
