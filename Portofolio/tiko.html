My Dream

3D ANIMATED SHORT FILM

Art Direction · Character Design · Animation · Layout & Compositing · Postproduction

with Cîrlig Tina, Casiadi Diamanta, Tacuș Vera

2025

My Dream is a character-driven 3D animated short exploring ambition, emotional tension, and reconciliation within a family.

A young penguin dreams of building a home for his family, but creative conflict and misunderstanding create distance instead of connection. As a storm gathers, isolation turns into realization, and the family must choose between pride and collaboration.

Through expressive animation and atmosphere-driven storytelling, the film reflects on how imagination can challenge authority — and how building something together ultimately rebuilds trust.

FRAMES

This short was developed collaboratively, with my role spanning multiple stages of production — from concept to final frame. I led the character design process, creating stylized low-poly characters focused on clarity, silhouette, and emotional readability. I handled UV mapping and texturing to ensure visual consistency across environments and lighting setups. I contributed to CG layout and the animatic phase, helping shape staging and visual rhythm. I animated key sequences with a focus on performance and emotional nuance, and completed the compositing and final edit to unify tone, atmosphere, and pacing. I also designed and animated the opening title and end credits sequences, creating all visual assets and motion design elements to maintain stylistic cohesion with the film’s world.
